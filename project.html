Welcome to Muzaffarpur, Bihar

Experience the charm of Muzaffarpur, a city nestled in the heart of Bihar, India. Known for its rich cultural heritage and vibrant traditions, Muzaffarpur offers a unique blend of history and modernity.

Discover the bustling markets, serene parks, and the warmth of the locals. Whether you're intrigued by historical landmarks or eager to savor local delicacies, Muzaffarpur has something for everyone.

Top Attractions

  1. Muzaffarpur Museum

    Explore the history and artifacts of Muzaffarpur in this renowned museum.

  2. Kalyani Devi Temple

    Visit this ancient temple known for its spiritual significance and architectural beauty.

  3. Gandhi Setu

    Walk across the iconic Gandhi Setu and enjoy panoramic views of the Ganges.
